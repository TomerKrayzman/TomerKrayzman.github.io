About

Hi! My name is Tomer Krayzman and I'm a senior at the University of Maryland College Park double majoring in Computer Science and Chinese. I was born in Israel to two parents who had left the Soviet Union, grew up in Baltimore, and currently live in College Park. I've been coding for many years and currently like working on front-end web development and game development. I do all of my game development in Unity using C#. I'm also interested in language learning — I currently know Russian, English, Hebrew, Spanish, and have been studying Chinese for over 3 years!
